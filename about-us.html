Our mission

Lombard Standard is a commercial real estate information marketplace designed to match buyers and sellers with minimum efforts and maximum confidentiality.

PRODUCT

Tech-enabled sourcing

We operate at the intersection between the Real Estate Fintech vertical (sub-sector) and the Information horizontal (driver). Our real estate platform is a fast, reliable and transparent infrastructure with confidentiality at its core to provide a safe and efficient environment.

Real Estate Fintech describes technology-based platforms which facilitate the trading of real estate asset owenrship. The assets can be buildings, shares or funds, debt or equity. The platforms may simply provide information for prospective buyers and sellers.- A. Baum, 2019
