LEADERSHIP

It's all possible with a great team

  • Alberto MG Saruggia

    Founder, CEO

    Alberto serves as CEO and is responsible for Lombard Standard’s overall direction and strategy. He founded the firm in 2018 after two decades in real estate developments among which Bulgari Hotel London, Park59 Tower New York and InterContinental Hong Kong.

  • Kelvin Ung

    Chief of Strategy

    Kelvin joined Lombard Standard in 2020 and primarily works on product consolidation, market positioning and digital innovation. Kelvin held senior executive roles at Liquidnet, CLSA, UBS and is a former regulator with the Hong Kong Securities and Futures Commission.

  • Norbert Bol

    Advisory Board

    Norbert is a key member of Lombard Standard's Advisory Board and provides fundamental industry insights, in terms of real estate investments, network reach and start-up development. He is a Member of the INREV Due Diligence Committee and former CIO of Sweco Capital.

  • Juay Hiang Tan

    Advisory Board

    Juay is instrumental to the firm's establishment and growth in South-East Asia, Singapore and Australia. He has a 15-year career as Managing Director at CapitaLand and Ascendas with exceptional knowledge of REIT investments, commercial properties and hospitality funds in the APAC region.

  • Tetsuro Matsumoto

    Advisory Board

    Tetsuro deploys a critical role in addressing the firm’s profitability and services in Japan. He has 30-year asset management experience with major corporations such as Bank of Tokyo and Citigroup, being engaged in capital markets, global equities, real estate securities and transactions.
