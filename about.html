Our business

The CRE buy/sell matching remains globally a complex legacy business where things are still done the old way: personal relationships. Sounds reassuring but it's widely known as an inefficient, costly and slow process that it's time to digitalize. The availability of AI provides now to any enterprise a level of computational power unimaginable just a few months ago. It's now possible to leverage algorithms at scale and with low costs. We believe that CRE matching will benefit greatly from this technology.
